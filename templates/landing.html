About Steganography

Steganography is the art and science of hiding information within other data. Unlike encryption, which makes data unreadable, steganography conceals the very existence of the message. Our platform uses advanced Least Significant Bit (LSB) modification techniques to embed messages within image pixels, making them undetectable to the naked eye.
